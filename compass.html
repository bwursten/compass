Scout's Guide: The Basics (Degree Reading)

Welcome, Scout! The first step is knowing how to set a specific 'bearing' on your dial. A bearing is just a degree number. The line at the very front of the compass is called the 'Index Line'. You use it to read or set your destination bearing.
